[h1] Infinite Ammo Plus [/h1]

[h2] Overview [/h2]

[b]Infinite Ammo Plus[/b] is a mod designed to enhance your Terraria experience by providing infinite ammunition options for various weapons. Say goodbye to running out of ammo during intense battles!

[h2] Features [/h2]

[list]
    [*] Infinite ammo for a wide range of weapons.
    [*] Balanced gameplay to ensure fair use of endless ammunition.
    [*] Easy to craft and use items for endless ammo.
[/list]

[h2] Compatibility [/h2]

This mod is compatible with most other mods and works seamlessly with the latest version of tModLoader.

[h2] Inspiration [/h2]

This mod was inspired by the [b]Endless Ammo Bags[/b] modification by UnVoicingCow. 
You can find their work here: [url]https://github.com/A-W031403/EndlessAmmoBags1.4.4.9[/url].
